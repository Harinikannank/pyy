MITHALI RAJ

  • Mithali Dorai Raj was born on 3rd December 1982 in Jodhpur,Rajasthan,India to a tamil family.
  • She aspired to become a bharathanatyam dancer and not a cricketer.
  • She started playing cricket at the age of ten.
  • she is the youngest centurion in ODI cricket at 16.
  • She became the 1st indian cricketer to score 2000 runs in T20 International Cricket.
  • She is an avid book reader.
  • Nicknamed as "Lady Tendulkar of Indian Women's Cricket".
  • She also got The Arjuna Award,The Padma Shri and The Khel Ratna Award.
